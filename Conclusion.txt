The PlantCare-AI: Advanced Plant Disease Detection Using Transfer Learning project successfully demonstrates how deep learning and transfer learning techniques can be applied to solve real-world agricultural problems. By leveraging a large-scale plant disease dataset and implementing a robust image classification pipeline, the system effectively identifies and classifies plant diseases from leaf images with high accuracy.
The final trained model achieved strong validation accuracy, demonstrating its ability to distinguish between healthy and diseased plant leaves. The integration of the trained model into a Flask-based web application further enhanced the practical applicability of the system. Users can upload leaf images through a simple web interface, and the model provides real-time predictions, making the solution accessible and user-friendly.
The long-term vision of PlantCare-AI is to evolve from a simple plant disease classifier into a comprehensive smart agriculture support system. By integrating artificial intelligence, mobile computing, cloud technology, and agricultural expertise, the system can play a significant role in:

Reducing crop losses

Increasing agricultural productivity

Supporting farmers with data-driven decisions

Promoting sustainable farming practices

With continuous development and real-world deployment, PlantCare-AI has the potential to become a scalable and impactful AI-driven agricultural solution.
